Project Overview

Info

For this recent project, I employed a combination of React, JavaScript, PHP, and CSS to craft a seamless website for Cozzi's Handmade Treats. Cozzi and I have a history of working together as teachers in the same classroom for an extended period, and it was a heartfelt experience to have the opportunity to create this website for her, which reflects the kindness and love she shares with everyone fortunate enough to call her a friend.

One aspect I particularly enjoyed was gaining proficiency in PHP and reinforcing my understanding of this technology. The website facilitates orders by sending notifications to both Cozzi and the user who initiated the order. Additionally, measures have been implemented to prevent an overwhelming influx of orders that could burden Cozzi's mailbox.

Should you wish to connect with Cozzi or stay updated with her offerings, please do not hesitate to reach out to her or follow her on Instagram. Her delightful treats are sure to bring joy to your day!
